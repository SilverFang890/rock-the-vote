Your Vote Makes a Difference

About two thirds (2/3, 66%) of elgible voters do not vote. Not only does this permit the government to take steps closer to ultimate power, it also works against the general population of the country. Would you mind the government controling your rent prices from now on (Prop 21)? Would you let the government take away suffrage from protestors who simply wanted change (Prop 17)? Even if you don't want to take to the streets to protest, VOTE in order to do your part in this democracy to control the government. WE, the citizens, control the government. Do not let it become the other way around.

What?

The 2020 United States Presidential Election

Why?

To take control of your government. Vote for the candidate who you feel represents you best. Vote for the propositions that will make your life easier.
