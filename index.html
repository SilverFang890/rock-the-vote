You Need To Vote

About two thirds (2/3, 66%) of elgible voters do not vote. Not only does this permit the government to take steps closer to hoarding power, it also works against the general population of the country. Would you mind the government controling your rent prices from now on (Prop 21)? Would you let the government take away the right of voting from protestors who simply wanted change (Prop 17)? Even if you don't want to take to the streets to protest, VOTE in order to do your part in this democracy to control the government. WE, the citizens, control the government. Do not let it become the other way around.
